Privacy Policy Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how information may be collected, used, stored, and shared when visitors use this website, submit a request form, call, email, or interact with platform features.

This website operates as an independent provider matching platform. It is not a water damage restoration company and does not perform restoration, drying, repair, cleanup, or remediation work directly.

Important platform distinction

Information submitted through this website may be used to help organize a request and connect visitors with independent local provider options. Independent providers are responsible for their own pricing, availability, timelines, estimates, documentation, warranties, licensing, insurance, and service decisions.

Information We May Collect

Visitors may provide information voluntarily when completing forms, clicking contact links, calling, emailing, or requesting provider options.

  • Name and contact information, such as phone number and email address.
  • ZIP code, city, state, or other location details related to a request.
  • Water damage category, damage type, timing, and optional request notes.
  • Information submitted through contact forms or intake fields.
  • Technical information such as device type, browser type, pages visited, and general interaction data.

How Information May Be Used

Information may be used to operate the website, respond to inquiries, improve the visitor experience, support request routing, and help visitors compare independent provider options.

  • To process and organize provider option requests.
  • To communicate with visitors about submitted inquiries.
  • To route request details based on ZIP code, service category, and submitted information.
  • To improve website functionality, forms, layout, and user experience.
  • To maintain security, prevent misuse, and support legal compliance.

Sharing Information With Independent Providers

When a visitor submits a request, information may be shared with independent local providers or service partners for the purpose of responding to the request or providing provider option information.

Independent providers are separate businesses. Their services, pricing, documentation, timing, warranties, licensing, insurance, and availability are not controlled by this website.

Cookies and Website Analytics

This website may use cookies, local storage, analytics tools, or similar technologies to improve site functionality, remember privacy preferences, understand user interactions, and improve performance.

More information is available in the Cookie Policy.

This website may contain links to third-party websites or may help visitors connect with independent providers. Third-party websites and providers operate under their own privacy practices and terms.

Visitors should review third-party policies directly before submitting information or hiring any provider.

Data Security

Visitor Choices
