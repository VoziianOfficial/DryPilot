Damage Area Mosaic

Burst pipe water can move through several interior zones.

The affected area may include the visible source and surrounding surfaces. Homeowners should compare how providers evaluate each area and document the request.

Ceiling water

Ask how providers review ceiling stains, sagging areas, water marks, and possible moisture spread.

Wall moisture

Ask what visible signs or readings may be included in the evaluation.

Flooring

Flooring material and exposure time can affect provider questions.

Cabinets, trim, and nearby rooms

Water may travel along surfaces or cavities. Ask providers what areas they inspect or document.
